# flow — movement (value/data in → out, conserved)

`flow` is the universal root of **movement**. Value moves between parties along the cart→order→invoice→payment chain ([[commerce]]); data moves in→out through the material cycle `1·2·4·8·7·5` ([[sequence]]); events move as ordered Lamport/Merkle streams; materials move through routing ([[manufacturing]]). What flows is conserved and accounted ([[accounting]], [[balance]]); status is *derived* from the flow's stage, never stored. The dual of [[balance]]: flow is movement, balance is movement at rest.

## In erpax
- The document chain ([[commerce]]); the data cycle ([[sequence]]); [[reconcile]] matches two sides of one flow; production movement ([[manufacturing]]).
