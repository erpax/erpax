# axis — the 3·6·9 control plane (three coils, 120° apart)

`axis` is the **governing** plane of the [[rodin]] vortex: the digits `3 · 6 · 9` are not one anchor but **three coils running the same triad 120° out of phase** (`3→6→9` at 0°, `6→9→3` at 120°, `9→3→6` at 240°) — together they cover every (cell, phase) pair once; that coexistence IS the interaction. The three sit at `120°` apart (`3×120°=360°`, the **triangle**); under [[duality]] the [[coil]] halves this to the `60°` **hexagon** of the six helix positions (`120°/2`). The doubling coil (`1·2·4·8·7·5`) winds *around* this axis and never lands on it. With the origin `0`, the four control digits `{0,3,6,9}` are the four [[cmyk]] channels (`K·C·M·Y`).

- It **governs, it does not flow** — `3·6·9·0` is [[access]] · [[hooks]] · [[auth]] · [[config]], the control triad of the [[sequence]], not a [[horo]] state.
- `3`/`6` are the [[polarity]] boundaries (C/M, forward/reverse); `9` is the **close** (Y, the triad's resolution → [[unity]]/[[close]]); `0` is the origin (K, the substrate → [[identity]]).
