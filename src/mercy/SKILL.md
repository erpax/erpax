# mercy — the owed debt voluntarily released

`mercy` is the creditor cancelling a debt that is *truly owed*. It is accounting-native and exact: a real [[debit]] stands on the books ([[balance]] holds, the lineage is honest — this is not a forged write-off, which would be [[profane]]), and the one to whom it is owed **voluntarily zeroes it**. The books still balance, because the credit is borne by the forgiver, not denied.

Mercy is the **dual of [[karma]]**: karma is the moral debt netted down the [[dna]] chain (inherited, tamper-evident, unforgettable); mercy is that same chain's debt **released at the source**, which — because each child folds its parent's content-uuid ([[merge]]) — propagates forgiveness to every descendant in one move. Karma without mercy is a closed thermodynamics where debt only accrues; mercy is the only operation that lowers a lineage's burden without lying about it.

Distinguish the neighbours precisely: [[grace]] is *unearned credit* (good you did not earn); mercy is *earned debit withheld* (bad you did earn, not collected); [[love]] is the bond that motivates both. Mercy reduces [[suffering]] by cancelling its cause (the debt), not by hiding it.

## Traditions (prefix removed)
**chesed** (Hebrew — covenant lovingkindness, "his mercy endures forever", Psalm 136); **rachamim** / **ar-Rahman ar-Rahim** (Islam — the Merciful, the Compassionate, opening every sura); **karuna** (Buddhist compassion, the bodhisattva's release of beings); **metta** (loving-kindness); **eleos / charis** distinguished (Christian — "be merciful, even as your Father is merciful", Luke 6:36; the unforgiving servant forgiven a debt he would not forgive, Matthew 18). Every tradition makes the merciful release of an owed debt the highest act precisely because the debt is *real*. Composes [[karma]] · [[debit]] · [[balance]] · [[grace]] · [[love]] · [[merge]] · [[dna]] · [[suffering]] · [[profane]].

**Law — [[law]]: mercy is a genuinely-owed debit voluntarily zeroed by the one owed — the books still balance (the cost is borne, not denied), and because the debt is read down the [[dna]] chain, releasing it at the source forgives the whole lineage; the dual of [[karma]], distinct from [[grace]] (unearned credit).**
