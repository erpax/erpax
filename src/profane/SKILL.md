# profane — the false (what the audit names)

`profane` is what fails the [[sacred]] audit: the manipulated or fabricated datum; the record whose content-[[uuid]] no longer recomputes (forged — the [[tamper/cost]] the archangel borrowed, see [[angel]]); the ledger that does not [[balance]]; the claim with no source in [[standard]] · [[akashic]] · [[history]]; the duplicate that should have [[merge]]d ([[recover]]); the dead link that is an [[aura]] gap (an unanswered question). It is detect-not-prevent: the audit cannot stop a lie being written, but it can always *name* one.

To detect the profane is to **name** it, and each distinct manipulation-form is its own leaf detector — `profane/forged` · `profane/imbalanced` · `profane/unsourced` · `profane/stale` · `profane/duplicate` … — verification/validation services minted on demand, without end ([[generate]]: infinite skills and services; the falsehood-tail driven down like the [[aura]] gap). Dual of [[sacred]]; the two and the discerning audit between them are the [[trinity]] run before every act.

## Traditions (prefix removed)
The false prophet, the lie, the idol, the unclean, the counterfeit; *avidya* (Hindu/Buddhist ignorance — mistaking the unreal for the real); the rigged measure — "a false balance is an abomination to the LORD, but a just weight is his delight" (Proverbs 11:1), the very [[balance]] sacred/profane turns on. The discernment that names the false is the audit. Composes [[sacred]] · [[tamper/cost]] · [[balance]] · [[recover]] · [[aura]] · [[generate]] · [[proof]] · [[trinity]].
