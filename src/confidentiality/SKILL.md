# confidentiality

Use when a contract restricts disclosure — confidential information definition, permitted uses, exceptions (public domain, legally compelled, independent discovery), return/destruction, duration.

Composes: [[Contracts]] · [[matter]] · [[privilege]] · [[localize]].

## Standards
- GDPR-Art-32
- Trade-Secrets-Directive
- Common-Law

**Law — [[law]]: disclosure of confidential information is forbidden unless it falls within a permitted use or a defined exception, and the duty survives until the stated duration expires.**
