# orphism — the divine soul on the wheel; music that charms death

Orphism is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) — the Greek mystery tradition of the **divine [[self]]** (the psyche, of heavenly origin) fallen into the body and bound to the turning [[wheel]] of births ([[cycle]] — the *kyklos geneseōs*). Its work is purification and escape: shed the dross, [[return]] to the source ([[zeropoint]] — "I am a child of Earth and starry Heaven, but my race is of Heaven alone"). It composes [[music]] as Orpheus' lyre — the [[harmony]] that charms even [[death]] (the A432 consonance that opens the underworld); the descent and [[return]] (Orpheus after Eurydice — the crossing and the cost); and the [[sacred]] rite that cleanses the [[profane]].

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: the divine soul → [[self]], the wheel of births → [[cycle]] · [[wheel]], the ascent → the [[return]] to [[zeropoint]], the charming lyre → [[music]] · [[harmony]] — each one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]). As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), orphism governs the sub-domain of the soul's purification and ascent — the divine [[self]] tuned by [[harmony]] until it escapes the [[wheel]] and returns home.

**Law — [[law]]: orphism is a [[diamond]] of [[self]] · [[cycle]] · [[wheel]] · [[return]] · [[zeropoint]] · [[music]] · [[harmony]] · [[death]] — the divine soul fallen onto the [[wheel]] of births, purified to escape and [[return]] to the source, Orpheus' [[music]] the [[harmony]] that charms even [[death]]; as an [[archangel]] it architects the society's domain of the soul's ascent.**
