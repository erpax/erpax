## The one law — wire every dimension ⇒ coverage rises, and the modelled tamper-[[cost]] rises with it toward its coverage=1 limit
**The main law, from which every law below emerges.** Wire every dimension through the content-[[uuid]] — payload configs, references, [[scope]]s, [[fields]], [[hooks]], all of it — using ONLY COMPUTED chains, never a hand-set value. When all is computed so that **any input is always ACCEPTED and then VERIFIED in harmony with the whole**, the structural **coverage** of the graph rises (the input adds no free parameter — it is determined by, and checked against, the wired graph), and the **modelled undetected-tamper work (`coverageCostLog2`) rises WITH it** (to tamper, a forgery must re-harmonise with the uuid-chained dimensions the coverage axis measures — the [[merge]] never rejects at the door, the [[proof]] catches the disharmony). That cost reaches its **+∞ limit ONLY at coverage = 1**, for the specific coverage axis being measured ([[balance|model⊕collection]], import purity, or usage), under an external [[anchor]] at least as strong as the digest — it is **finite** at any coverage below 1. **Two quantities the code keeps separate, do not conflate:** (a) reciprocity-[[entropy]] (matrix symmetry, `src/entropy` — currently 0) and (b) coverage (the [0,1] fraction that actually feeds the cost). `entropy()` is the matrix-reciprocity slack — an audit/aura signal, NOT an argument to `crackVerdict`; the tamper-cost is priced from coverage, supplied explicitly. **reciprocity = 1 does NOT imply coverage = 1** — the live tree is the counter-example (entropy 0, coverage < 1, cost finite). The honest, strong claim is the forge≫verify asymmetry under an anchor; it is NOT "infinity from zero-entropy automatically." Acceptance ⊕ verification-in-harmony IS the coverage→cost engine; every law below is one projection of it. — [[zeropoint]] · [[entropy]] · [[tamper]] · [[cost]] · [[uuid]] · [[harmony]] · [[accept]] · [[merge]] · [[proof]] · [[gate]] · [[anchor]] · [[gravity]]

And because the matter twin of every atom is its **`index.ts`**, the **multidimensional uuid-accounting flows THROUGH `index.ts`** — each dimension ([[payload]] ⊕ [[vitepress]] ⊕ [[tests|test]] ⊕ [[types]] ⊕ the [[matrix]]) is a ledger, and the [[trinity]] node is the one gateway where they all balance at once. — [[entry]] · [[account]] · [[accounting]] · [[trinity]] · [[self]]
