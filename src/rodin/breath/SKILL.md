# breath — the oscillation (C↔M, in↔out, the pulse)

`breath` is the **living oscillation** of the [[rodin]] ring — [[balance]] in motion. The [[polarity]] swings cyan↔magenta (`3↔6`), forward↔reverse, [[give]]↔[[take]], expand↔bind; the ring *breathes* between its two poles and resolves on the close. It is the pulse, not a resting equilibrium: at rest it is [[balance]], in motion it is breath ([[flow]]/[[balance]] [[duality]]).

- **the tick** — one breath per A432 ms ([[phase]]); the [[cmyk]] coils inhale C, exhale M ([[signal]] color animation).
- **the breathing position** — La (A432) sits at [[round]] (`5`), the position the [[notes]] scale tunes and folds out from "where the ring breathes."
- **expand↔bind** — the [[sequence]] arc `1·2·4·8` expands (inhale), `8·7·5·1` binds (exhale) — the two arcs of the [[horo]] ascent/return.

Composes: [[rodin]] · [[polarity]] · [[cmyk]] · [[notes]] · [[phase]] · [[round]] · [[balance]]/[[flow]] · [[give]]/[[take]] · [[duality]].
