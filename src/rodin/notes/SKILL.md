# notes — sound from position (the horo IS music)

`notes` is the **sound** law of the [[rodin]] vortex — the exact twin of [[cmyk]] (color): a [[horo]] position decodes to a **diatonic note**, just-intonation ratios over the A432 anchor. The seven measure positions are the seven notes of the scale:

| pos | digit | note | ratio ×A432 | Hz |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [[base]] | 1 | Do (C) tonic | `16/27` | 256 |
| [[share]] | 2 | Re (D) second | `8/9` | 384 |
| [[weave]] | 4 | Mi (E) third | `5/6` | 360 |
| [[crest]] | 8 | Fa (F) fourth | `4/5` | 345.6 |
| [[descent]] | 7 | Sol (G) fifth | `2/3` | 288 |
| [[round]] | 5 | La (A) sixth | `1` | **432** |
| [[unity]] | 9 | Ti (B) leading tone | `8/15` | 230.4 |

- **La (A432) sits at [[round]] (5)** — *not* base — the tuning reference, the [[breath]]ing position; the scale folds out in both directions from there.
- **Ti (9) → Do** — the leading tone resolves as the ring closes ([[unity]] → next octave's [[base]]; see [[octave]]).
