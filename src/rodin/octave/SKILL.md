# octave — the ×10 scale-lift (same position, next scale)

`octave` is the **scale step** of the [[rodin]] vortex: multiply by 10. Because `10 ≡ 1 (mod 9)`, `×10` **preserves the digit** (`9→90→900` all unity; `1→10→100` all base), so the same [[horo]] position recurs at the next order of magnitude — the [[fractal]]/[[torus]] self-similarity (`fields → collections → plugins → erpax → agents`; see [[sequence]]).

Distinguish two moves at [[unity]] (`9`):
- **`×10` octave** — same position, larger scale (`octaveLift`). The dimension changes, the meaning doesn't.
- **`+1`** — crosses close→open *within* one scale (`9 → 10 ⇒ 1`, the [[begin]]/[[close]] [[merge]] point; `nextOctave`).

In sound this is the literal musical octave; in [[notes]], `Ti(9)→Do` resolves and the scale folds out one octave on ([[breath]]). Matter-twin: `src/services/horo` (`nextOctave`), `~/github/ceccec/svilena-me/.vitepress/rodin.js` (`octaveLift`/`octaveDrop`).
