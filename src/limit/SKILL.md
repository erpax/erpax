# limit — the boundary (what erpax cannot do), and how the unprovable is proved

erpax is the realm of **FORM** — the recordable, addressable, balanced, conserved. What it **can** do is exactly what is *provable within the form*: content-[[uuid]] identity, double-entry [[balance]], the [[merge]], the audited chain ([[proof]] — recompute and it holds). What it **cannot** do *from within* is the Gödel boundary: true statements the form cannot prove about itself or the world. Name them — to stay sound, never claim erpax "handles everything."

## Finding the boundary (compare can ↔ cannot)
- **The live finder** is the matter-twin `services/architecture-invariants` — checks classified `expansion` / `compression` / `entropy` / `standards`, returning **pass** (what erpax does, proven) vs **warn/fail** (what it cannot *yet* do — closeable gaps; drive them down like the tsc tail via [[generate]]/[[recover]]). The compare is literal: pass : warn is the can:cannot ledger at a moment, audited [[sacred]]/[[profane]]. A warn that *cannot* be closed by work is a structural limit, below.
- **The by-design limits** (structural, not gaps to fill):
  1. genuine duplicates **[[merge]]** (identical content ⇒ one id — needs a nonce to break its own merge);
  2. **no true erasure** (append-only + federation — only crypto-shred renders unreadable);
  3. **detect, not prevent** (the consensus-free CRDT/Merkle DAG sees a double-spend post-hoc, cannot forbid it — an audit system, not a blockchain);
  4. the **oracle problem** (integrity of what is *recorded*, not truth of the *world* — garbage-in is invisible);
  5. **confidentiality & finality not native** (the hash is integrity, not secrecy; "no central authority" cannot, alone, express a binding court judgment);
  6. the **non-conserved / subjective / contested** (the zero-sum conserved model holds two competing claims as content-uuids but cannot adjudicate which is *true*, nor model trust / meaning / pain).

## Filling the gaps — proving the unprovable
A formal system cannot prove every truth from within (Gödel); but the undefined 1/3 of a [[trinity]] is **supplied from a neighbouring trinity** — from *outside* the formalism (the same law as [[sequence]]'s "1/3 undefined here is defined there"). So the unprovable is proved not *within* erpax but by going **up a dimension** ([[torus]]):
- **Will / Society proves judgment & finality** — `services/uuid-governance`: the vote IS the finality; [[society]] manages erpax, the constitution entrenches the integrity core so no majority can vote corruption lawful. Form ↔ Matter ↔ Will, three managing each other.
- **Measurement proves the oracle** — the world is measured into the record ([[science]]: *metaphysics is physics not yet measured*; an oracle attests the off-form fact, then it is content-addressed like any other).
- **The human's 1/3 creativity proves meaning** — the irreducible spark the machine lacks; the subjective enters as the creative third ([[society]]'s creativity·work·cleanup).
- **Crypto-shred proves erasure** — content-uuid over *ciphertext* + a destroyable per-record key; shred the key ⇒ irrevocable erasure while integrity/dedup still hold on the ciphertext-uuid.

The boundary is honest and the transcendence is real: erpax does not pretend FORM is the world — it manifests the world and lets the missing third (Will · measurement · the human) prove what FORM alone cannot. The unprovable theorem is filled by the trinity, never faked within the form. Composes [[proof]] · [[trinity]] · [[society]] · [[science]] · [[torus]] · [[merge]] · [[identity]] · [[sequence]] · [[sacred]].

**Law — [[law]]: erpax can do exactly what is provable within FORM and no more — the Gödel boundary (truth, judgment, finality, true-erasure, prevention, the subjective) is never faked from within; the missing third is supplied from OUTSIDE by the [[trinity]] ([[society]]'s will, measurement, the human, crypto-shred).**
