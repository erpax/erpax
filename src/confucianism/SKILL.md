# confucianism — ordered relations; let the name match the thing

Confucianism is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) composed of invariant sub-diamonds, ordered toward [[harmony]] (**he** — the supreme social good) through cultivated relation. Its keystone is the most erpax-native idea in any tradition: **zhengming**, the *rectification of [[name]]s* — "let the ruler be a ruler, the father a father", let the [[name]] match the thing it names. That IS the [[identity]] law (name = content, content-[[uuid]] = self); to govern is to restore the binding of name to reality, exactly as the corpus restores it. It is a lattice: [[love]] as **ren** (humaneness, benevolence — the central virtue, "do not do to others what you would not have done to you"); [[ritual]] / [[order]] as **li** (propriety, the right enacted ordering of relations on the [[cycle]]); [[justice]] as **yi** (righteousness, doing what is fitting); [[wisdom]] as **zhi**; [[faith]] as **xin** (trustworthiness, the word kept); and the [[self]] cultivated as the root from which family, state, and world order outward ([[fractal]], the same rule at every scale).

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: strip the Chinese prefix and **ren** → [[love]], **li** → [[ritual]] / [[order]], **yi** → [[justice]], **zhi** → [[wisdom]], **xin** → [[faith]], **zhengming** → [[name]] / [[identity]] — each resolving to one content-[[uuid]], so Confucianism names the same atoms in Chinese ([[merge]] at the root, [[harmony]] at the surface).

As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), Confucianism governs the domain of [[name]] and ordered relation — and is in a sense the society's archetypal [[architect]]: zhengming (the rectification of [[name]]s) IS the [[identity]] law that lets the lattice cohere at all, so it composes the sub-diamonds into the very structure of accountable relation, shaping the lattice the same way across millennia ([[fractal]]).

**Law — [[law]]: confucianism is a [[diamond]] of [[harmony]] · [[name]] · [[identity]] · [[love]] · [[ritual]] · [[order]] · [[justice]] · [[wisdom]] · [[faith]] · [[self]] — relations ordered toward he ([[harmony]]) whose keystone is zhengming, the rectification of [[name]]s (name = thing = the [[identity]] law), the [[self]] cultivated outward at every scale ([[fractal]]); each term strips its prefix to one content-[[uuid]] ([[translator]]).**
