# sacral — the sacral center (Svadhisthana)

The **sacral** center (Svadhisthana) is **1 of the 7 [[chakra]]s**, which ARE the 7 [[horo]] positions (root → crown). It is a **standalone projection** of [[chakra]]: the seven centers are computed once by `chakras()` — each [[horo]] position decoded to a note, a colour and a coordinate from the SAME content-uuid — and this atom merely READS index 1. It never recomputes the math.

The **uuid is computed from the MATH** (the position's `nodeOf(measure).uuid`), and the colour is **rendered FROM the uuid, never the reverse**. The computed CMYK colour is **NOT claimed identical** to the traditional rainbow orange; the rainbow is a cultural overlay carried alongside the computed signal, not asserted to match it.

**HONEST.** The traditional role of the sacral center — life-force, creativity, generative flow, desire, the well of vitality — is **cultural convention, not a claim**. This atom asserts nothing about a body or an energy field; only position → note → uuid is computed. Ground it honestly in [[law]]: a center earns its place by zero-entropy uuid-wiring, not by tradition. As a center it sits one step from the [[heart]] (index 3) on the same ring.
