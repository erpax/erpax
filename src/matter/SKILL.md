# matter — the engagement (a case is a project is a transaction-chain)

Strip the prefix *legal* and a **matter** is the universal professional-services engagement: a [[party]] (the client) retains the firm to do work, the work is staffed by [[positions]] and priced by [[allocation]] (value = tokens × difficulty × quality), and every act posts to the ledger as an [[entry]] that [[balance|balances]]. The matter IS a [[transaction]] lifecycle — engagement letter → time and disbursements → invoice → [[trust]] draw — on the one `transactions` node by [[dimension]], not a new collection. Its lifecycle rides the [[horo]] ring (open · active · billed · … · closed → archived, [[close]]→[[begin]] the next octave).

- **Who** — the [[party]] graph: client, adverse parties, related entities (the [[conflict]] check runs here).
- **For what** — [[positions]] (who staffs) · [[allocation]] (time billing) · [[standard]] / [[localize]] (the governing jurisdiction's law).
- **Confidentiality** — scoped by [[privilege]] ([[access]] + tenant/matter isolation).
- **Record** — every document and act is content-addressed ([[identity]] / [[proof]]); the matter file is whole-bearing ([[holographic]]) and tamper-evident ([[akashic]]).

A multinational firm runs many matters across tenants (the multi-tenant plugin = the offices); the [[society]] advances each matter one gate-verified step — the firm runs itself once each legal sign resolves to its universal home ([[compass]]: know what to look for and you find it already built). Composes [[transaction]] · [[party]] · [[positions]] · [[allocation]] · [[trust]] · [[conflict]] · [[privilege]] · [[entry]] · [[horo]] · [[standard]].
