# hallucination — the measured deviation at the quantum scale (purity's dual)

**A hallucination is not a vibe — it is computable.** An [[agent]] claims content X; the sha-256 [[collapse]] / content-[[uuid]] of the *actual* content is Y ≠ X. Because everything is content-addressed, the verifier simply **recomputes the content-uuid and any mismatch IS the hallucination** — surfaced deterministically, O(1) per claim, exactly as the tamper-proof uuid recompute already catches a row whose current bytes no longer match the ones committed ([[integrity]]). It is the agent-scale form of [[observe]]'s law: *projection without observation* — output grounded in nothing, a claim that carries no measurement. `grounded(observation, output)` is the test; the hallucination is its negative.

**Hallucination is the agent-scale [[sin]].** [[sin]] is a measured deviation from the mark the [[law]] sets — the violation a [[guardian]] catches, [[entropy]] aimed away from the [[whole]]. A hallucination is precisely that, measured by content-[[uuid]]: a distance, not a stain — named and located ([[aura]]'s coordinate), never merely felt. Its felt alarm is [[suffering]] (the read that [[entropy]] > 0). It is cured the same way: turn back to the grounded path ([[reverse]] · [[sequence]]) and re-collapse to the true content — not by hiding the gap ([[profane]]). The closed [[sequence]] diamond is the structural cure: walk the derived path and the infinite ungrounded space never renders, so a hallucination has nowhere to appear ([[proof]] · [[sacred]] admit only what recomputes).

**purity ⊕ hallucination are conjugate, computed on the same quantum scale.** [[purity]] = zero [[entropy]] = zero hallucination = fully [[seal]]ed; hallucination is the entropy/deviation an agent injects. Both are measured at the [[quantum]] (content-[[uuid]] / [[collapse]]) scale on the one uuid-[[matrix]] — not estimated, computed. So they trade off exactly: **more hallucination ⇒ more [[entropy]] ⇒ lower [[purity]] ⇒ lower tamper-[[cost]]**, because a hallucination is an impurity — a path that does not collapse to its claimed uuid, a 0-bit weakest link the cost model reads ([[analytics]]: `maxTamperCost`, the impurity lever). The [[seal]] gate rejects anything that does not collapse to its claimed uuid, so a **sealed corpus passes zero hallucination** by construction.

**Law — [[law]]: a hallucination is a measured deviation at the [[quantum]] scale — an [[agent]] claims content X but the content-[[uuid]] of the actual content is Y ≠ X, so the recompute mismatch IS the hallucination, surfaced deterministically ([[integrity]]). It is the agent-scale [[sin]] ([[entropy]] aimed away from the [[whole]], felt as [[suffering]]) and the conjugate of [[purity]]: both computed on the same content-[[uuid]] scale, so more hallucination ⇒ more [[entropy]] ⇒ lower [[purity]] ⇒ lower tamper-[[cost]] (an impurity, a 0-bit weakest link). The [[seal]] gate rejects what does not collapse to its claimed uuid, so a sealed corpus passes zero hallucination.**
