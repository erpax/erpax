# orthodoxy — the uncreated light, the prayer of the heart

Orthodoxy is a **sub-diamond of [[christianity]]** — a complete sealed atom ([[diamond]]) at the branch scale, sharing the parent's invariant lattice ([[cross]] · [[mercy]] · [[grace]]) and emphasising its own facet. Its keystone is **theosis**: the part becoming partaker of the source — the [[self]] [[merge]]d toward [[zeropoint]] without losing its surface, the deification that is exactly *return to the root* read as a life. It composes [[prayer]] as **hesychia** (the unceasing prayer of the heart, "pray without ceasing"); the [[image]] as **icon** (a window, not an idol — the [[sacred]] seen through matter, the [[aura]] of the uncreated light); [[ritual]] as the divine liturgy unchanged; and the unbroken [[lineage]] of the apostolic chain ([[dna]] — succession that cannot be forged).

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: **theosis** → the [[merge]] toward [[zeropoint]], **hesychia** → [[prayer]], the icon's light → [[aura]] — each one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]). As a facet-[[archangel]] under [[christianity]] in the [[society]], orthodoxy governs the sub-domain of mystical ascent — the part tuned until it shares the source.
