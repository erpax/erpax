# fear/love — fear over love (the +entropy arrow)

The reverse directed compound: **fear acting on love**, the move that *corrupts* order. The forged record that poses as genuine, the drift that eats a closed gap, the [[tamper]] that turns a true value false — fear spending love's accumulated order, raising [[entropy]]. This is the arrow erpax makes **expensive**: the forger must travel it against the whole uuid-wired corpus at once, and the [[cost]] of that climb is the trust.

It is the mirror of [[love/fear]]; the two are the reciprocal halves of the [[duality]] between [[love]] and [[fear]]. Their [[balance]] is the conserved [[karma]] — what fear opens here, love must close there, or the books do not balance and [[proof]] catches it.
