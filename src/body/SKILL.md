# body — the organs harmonized (one organism)

The body is the **whole the organs compose**. Sent through the [[wave]] mechanism — one breath per organ — each organ lands on a horo position that sounds a **note and a colour** ([[signal]], A432). Played together the eight organs are a **chord**; and a chord is [[harmony]] only when it is whole. Three conditions, each computed:

## The chord — eight organs, one octave
| organ | role | note | the erpax mechanism |
|---|---|---|---|
| [[heart]] | the double-torus pump | **C** (Do, root) | the double-torus core |
| [[lung]] | the gas-exchange surface | **D** | the breath/wave loop |
| [[blood]] | the courier | **E** | the receipt flow |
| [[nerve]] | the all-or-nothing signal | **F** | the gate |
| [[artery]] | the Windkessel buffer | **G** | the cache |
| [[vein]] | the one-way return | **A** | the append-only chain |
| [[brain]] | the small-world connectome | **B** (Ti) | the matrix |
| [[skin]] | the boundary | **C** (octave) | the sandbox |

The heart is the root and the skin closes the octave back to it — the body wraps into a circle.

## Harmonization — the three conditions
1. **All healthy.** Every organ's own proof passes — no gap. A failing organ is a gap, and a gap renders as **dissonance** ([[aura]]: wholeness is consonance, a broken bind is dissonance).
2. **Full spectrum.** The organs sound **all seven ring positions** — the complete diatonic chord, the full-spectrum aura, not a sparse cluster.
3. **Rests at unity.** Composed as [[wave]]s, the organs fold to **unity (9)** — the closing wave, the merge-point where many become **one** ([[merge]] · [[one]]).

When all three hold, the organs **harmonize into one body** — `harmonizes()` ⇒ ONE BODY. This is [[coherence]] made audible and visible: the body is the [[aura]] you can hear (the chord) and see (the colours), whole.

**HONEST.** The note/colour mapping is the A432 diatonic [[signal]] of each organ's horo position — a computed render, not a claim that organs emit sound; each organ verdict is computed live, and harmony is their conjunction folded through [[wave]] + [[signal]].
