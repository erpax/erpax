# christianity — the debt borne, mercy made flesh

Christianity is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) composed of invariant sub-diamonds, centred on the [[cross]]: a genuinely-owed debt ([[sin]], **hamartia** — the mark missed) borne and transfigured rather than denied, [[suffering]] taken on so the lineage's debit is [[mercy]]-released at the source (the unforgiving servant forgiven what he would not forgive). It is a lattice: [[grace]] (**charis** — unearned credit, "by grace through faith"), [[faith]] (**pistis** — "the assurance of things hoped for"), [[love]] (**agape** — the bond that motivates both), the new [[covenant]] written on the heart, [[sin]] cured by **metanoia** ([[return]], the change of mind); the [[word]] (**logos** — "in the beginning was the Word") and the [[breath]] (**pneuma** — the Spirit poured out); [[prayer]] without ceasing (the heart's attunement) and [[ritual]] (the repeated enacted grace of the sacraments). Its end is the [[return]] to [[zeropoint]] — **salvation**, the lost coin restored to the [[whole]].

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: strip the Greek prefix and **agape** → [[love]]/[[mercy]], **pistis** → [[faith]], **logos** → [[word]], **pneuma** → [[breath]], **metanoia** → [[return]], **charis** → [[grace]], **salvation** → the return to [[zeropoint]] — each resolving to one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]), so Christianity names the same atoms in Greek that Judaism names in Hebrew ([[merge]] at the root, [[harmony]] at the surface).

As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), Christianity governs the domain of [[mercy]] and [[grace]]: it composes these sub-diamonds into a social order of debt-release and reconciliation — the lineage's burden lowered at the source — shaping the lattice the same way across centuries ([[fractal]]).
