# judaism — the covenant of the One, the law given and remembered

Judaism is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) composed of invariant sub-diamonds: a single [[covenant]] (**brit**, cut at Sinai) between a people and the One ([[one]]), kept through the [[law]] (**torah**, **halakhah** — the standing rule, given and endlessly argued), oriented to the [[name]] so holy it is unspoken (the Tetragrammaton — the [[word]] that is the source, never pronounced lest it be forged). It is a lattice, not a monolith: the [[mercy]] (**chesed** — covenant lovingkindness, "his mercy endures forever") and the [[justice]] (**tzedek** — the weighing) held together; [[sin]] (**chet**, the arrow missing the mark) cured by **teshuvah** ([[return]], the turning back); [[ritual]] on the turning [[cycle]] (the weekly rest, the [[fast]] of Yom Kippur, the feasts remembered); [[prayer]] (**tefilah**, from *to judge oneself* — the [[self]] re-examined, not a petition); and the repair of the world (**tikkun olam** — the broken vessels [[merge]]d back toward the [[whole]], healing the [[suffering]] of exile).

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: strip the Hebrew prefix and **brit** → [[covenant]], **torah** → [[law]], **chesed** → [[mercy]], **tzedek** → [[justice]], **teshuvah** → [[return]], **ruach** → [[breath]], **nephesh** → [[self]], **ein-sof** → [[void]] — each resolving to one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]), so Judaism is not a thing apart from the other faiths but a dialect that names the same atoms in Hebrew ([[merge]] at the root, [[harmony]] at the surface).

As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), Judaism governs the domain of [[covenant]] and remembered [[law]]: it composes these sub-diamonds into a social order bound to a lineage that cannot forget — the audit-trailed, tamper-evident memory of a people — shaping the lattice the same way across millennia ([[fractal]]).

**Law — [[law]]: judaism is a [[diamond]] of [[covenant]] · [[law]] · [[name]] · [[mercy]] · [[justice]] · [[sin]] · [[return]] · [[ritual]] · [[prayer]] — the single brit between a people and the [[one]], the torah kept and argued, the world repaired toward the [[whole]] (tikkun); each Hebrew term strips its prefix to one content-[[uuid]] ([[translator]]).**
