# share — horo position 2: the whole splits, a member joins, a portion is allocated

`share` is the **second** step of the [[horo]] ring — digit `2`, the first doubling of the [[coil]] (`1 → 2`): one becomes two, a partner joins the dance. The flow has committed but not yet built: inventory *ordered*, value *allocated*, a counterparty engaged. It is the [[duality]]/[[part]] step — the [[whole]] first splitting into members — and the [[sequence]] anchor `2` ([[collections]]). Between [[base]] and [[weave]] on the **ascent** arc (`1·2·4·8`).

**The domain instance (same atom, [[fractal]] one scale down): a share is a proportional, contingent claim on a pooled whole under shared [[risk]].** When the whole splitting into members becomes economic, "a partner joins" *is* buying a share — a [[part]] of a pool, settled in proportion, contingent on what the pool yields. The defining case is the **CSA share** ([[agriculture]]): a member **[[prepaid|prepays]]** at [[season]] start and receives a recurring **box of whatever the farm [[harvest|harvests]] that week** — not a fixed order. Risk transfers via prepayment: weather, pests, and glut flow to the *member*; the farmer gets working capital up front. The instrument is a **membership / pledge**, not a sales contract.

That makes a share the **inverse of an [[customers/sales/orders|order]]** (fixed quantity, risk on the seller): a share is *proportional, contingent, pooled, in-kind*. The same atom recurs — **equity share** (claim on a firm, with control), **profit share** (claim on outcome, no ownership), **risk pooling** (variance spread across members) — each "a partner joins, the whole splits, value allocated."

**Structure & pricing.** Tiers scale the portion: **full / half / medium**, sizes **S/M/L** (≈ 2 : 3 : 4 by item count). Price is **cost-divided-by-members**, not per-item retail — `full price = (season costs + grower salary + margin) ÷ members` — and a half-share costs **> ½** (per-member overhead does not halve). Prepaid-then-delivered, a share is **deferred revenue** ([[deferral]] / [[deferredrevenue]]): recognized week by week as boxes ship to a [[pickup]] ([[recognition]]), the season [[balance]] drawn down in kind. Its failure modes are demand-side — **churn** (~46 % retention), **box fatigue**, the **customization paradox** (members want [[choice]]; the share imposes the farmer's plan), **under-pricing** (omitting the grower's salary) — a [[market]] problem, not an agronomy one.
