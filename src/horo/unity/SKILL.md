# unity — horo position 9 (the ring closes)

`unity` is the **close** of the [[horo]] ring — digit `9`, the absorbing step where every flow resolves: inventory *settled*, a period *closed*, a document *sealed*, two sides *reconciled* at rest. It is the [[close]]/[[end]]/[[one]] of a [[flow]] expressed as a ring position. On the [[coil]] it absorbs (any `×9 ⇒ 9`, any `×0 ⇒ 9`) and **mirror-twins the next octave's [[base]]** (`9 → 10 ⇒ 1`): a close IS the next ring's open — the [[merge]] / [[begin]] point (the accounting close→open, the seal→begin). Last of the **return** arc (`7·5·9`); its right hand reaches back to [[base]], closing the circle.

**Law — [[law]]: [[horo]] position 9 — the absorbing close where every flow resolves (settled · sealed · reconciled) and which mirror-twins the next octave's [[base]]: a close IS the next ring's open (the [[merge]] point).**
