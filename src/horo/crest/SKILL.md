# crest — horo position 8 (the ascent peaks)

`crest` is the **peak** of the [[horo]] ring — digit `8`, the last doubling of the [[coil]] (`4 → 8`) and the top of the **ascent** arc (`1·2·4·8`). The flow is fully built and ready to turn: inventory *packed*, an order *assembled*, the multiverses **merge** here ([[sequence]] anchor `8` — [[queries]]/[[tags]], "at 8 the architectures merge"). The next doubling folds back down (`8 → 16 ⇒ 7`), beginning the return. The high point before [[descent]]; holds [[weave]] on its left.
