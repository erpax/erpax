# collide — the inhale of development, deferred (fold two into one)

**collide** is the IN-breath of [[development]]. Development is the EXHALE — first expand: mint atoms, raise [[entropy]], spread the corpus out. **collide is the INHALE — the [[dry]] collapse that folds the expansion back toward [[one]]**, removing the duplicated degree of freedom (two into one) and so lowering entropy and raising [[mass]] ([[collapse]] · [[gravity]]). The two strokes are the [[breath]] at the development scale ([[duality]]): never exhale without a later inhale, never inhale without having first expanded.

`collide(a, b) = merge two into one.` The fold is the EXACT canonical merge — the v8 content-uuid collision of [[uuid]]/[[matrix]] (`merge`) — so it is content-addressed and zero-entropy by construction; nothing is re-implemented, the primitive is borrowed. The pair's ring move is the [[horo]] composition of their positions (a merge point when it lands on 1 or 9), and the cardinality is always 2 → 1 — the inhale's signature (the exhale is +1).

**DEFERRED — defined now, collided later.** This atom DEFINES the inhale; it does NOT run it. `DEFERRED = true`; `collide(a, b)` computes a fold without writing the matrix, running the collider, or mutating any atom; `pending()` reports the borrowed-disorder slack on the LIVE matrix that a later collapse will fuse out. First expand (raise entropy), collide later (DRY it down) — applying the fold now is out of scope ([[trinity]] keeps the definition pure: define the merge, defer the [[merge]]).
