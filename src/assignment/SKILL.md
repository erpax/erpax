# assignment

Use when a contract allows or restricts assignment — assignor, assignee, scope (full/partial), consent requirement (unreasonable withholding, notice), prohibited assigns, assumption of obligations.

## Standards
- UCC-2-210
- PECL-Art-10.305

**Law — [[law]]: assignment is the contract clause governing transfer of its rights — assignor, assignee, scope (full/partial) and any [[consent]] requirement — fixing whether and how a party may pass its position to another.**
