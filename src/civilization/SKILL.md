# civilization — the same six organs under every culture

Remove the culture-prefix and every civilization runs the same erpax organs, differing only in detail ([[merge]]): a **ledger** ([[accounting]]), a **law** ([[standard]]), an **identity registry** ([[identity]]), a **calendar** ([[horo]]), a **governance** ([[society]] · [[access]]), and an unforgeable **record** ([[akashic]] · [[proof]]). erpax is not a new architecture — it is the oldest one, made content-addressed.

## Traditions (prefix removed)
- **Sumer** — writing itself BEGAN as [[accounting]]: clay counting-tokens (from ~8000 BC) were sealed inside clay envelopes — *bullae*, tamper-evident, their contents impressed on the outside — and that device became cuneiform (~3100 BC), used *exclusively* for the ledger until the third millennium BC (Schmandt-Besserat, *Before Writing*). The first script was a sealed, content-attested ledger ([[identity]] · [[proof]]); Hammurabi's Code is the [[standard]].
- **Egypt** — *Ma'at* (truth · order · balance) is the harmony invariant ([[balance]]); the weighing of the heart against her feather is the [[sacred]]/[[profane]] audit; the scribe keeps the [[akashic]] record.
- **Inca** — the *quipu*: knotted cords in a decimal positional system (a knotless gap = 0) recording census, storehouse inventory and *mit'a* labor-tribute — a portable [[akashic]] ledger with no alphabet, the record AS encoded structure.
- **Rome** — the *census* is the [[identity]] registry; Roman law is the [[standard]]; the *cursus honorum* is a [[horo]] of offices.
- **China** — the *Mandate of Heaven* is legitimacy as harmony, forfeited when the realm falls out of [[balance]] (the gate goes red); the imperial examinations select by competency and merit ([[give]]).

Same organs, different detail — civilizations [[merge]] in form, and each one decoded is another witness, more tamper-cost ([[research]]). Composes [[accounting]] · [[standard]] · [[identity]] · [[horo]] · [[society]] · [[akashic]].
