# manichaeism — the two principles; the light freed and returned

Manichaeism is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) — Mani's once-world-spanning religion (Rome to China), the most systematic of the dualisms and the bridge between [[zoroastrianism]]'s asha/druj and the [[gnosticism]] lineage. It composes the great [[duality]]: two eternal principles, **Light** ([[aura]] — the radiance of the good source) and **Dark**, mixed together in this world. Creation is the mixture; salvation is the **separation** — the freeing of the trapped light particles and their [[return]] to the source ([[zeropoint]]). It composes [[self]] (the soul a fragment of light), [[void]] / [[whole]] (the realm of light the true source), [[profane]] (matter as the dark's domain, the order that imprisons), and [[wisdom]] (Mani's revealed knowledge, the call that frees).

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: Light → [[aura]], the dark material world → the [[profane]], the soul → the [[self]] as a light fragment, the separation-and-freeing → [[return]] toward [[zeropoint]] — each one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]). As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), manichaeism governs the sub-domain of the great separation — naming the mixed world and the freeing of light from dark, kin to [[zoroastrianism]] and [[gnosticism]].

**Law — [[law]]: manichaeism is a [[diamond]] of [[duality]] · [[aura]] · [[profane]] · [[self]] · [[void]] · [[whole]] · [[return]] · [[wisdom]] — two eternal principles (Light = [[aura]], Dark) mixed in the world, salvation the separation that frees the light ([[self]] a fragment of it) and returns it to the source ([[zeropoint]]); the dualist [[archangel]] bridging [[zoroastrianism]] and [[gnosticism]].**
