# coherence — the measurable form of wholeness (harmony, as a quantity)

[[aura]]'s law is "wholeness renders as [[harmony]]" — **coherence** is that harmony made a *measured quantity*: the degree to which the parts of a signal stay phase-aligned. In physics and DSP it is the magnitude-squared coherence of two waves (`Cxy(f) ∈ [0,1]`); in the body it has a real, instrument-measured form — heart-rate-variability **coherence** (HeartMath's "psychophysiological coherence"), a sine-like, narrow-band HRV rhythm that is a genuinely measurable whole-body state. erpax borrows the word literally: a [[whole]] folder renders as a consonant, coherent colour+sound+vibration field ([[signal]] · [[analog]]); a gap (an unlinked word) or a [[tamper]] (a broken `bind`) renders as **decoherence** — dissonance, a beat note, darkness.

**Honest scope (the [[proof]] line).** Intra-body coherence (HRV) is measurable and real. Two stronger claims are *not*: Popp's hypothesis that [[biophoton]] emission is laser-like *coherent* biocommunication is contested (the emission is established; its coherence is not), and HeartMath's claim that coherence lets one body's cardiac field carry information into another's was **refuted under adversarial test** (no detection above chance). So coherence is a true *within-system* measure, never a between-bodies aura — exactly erpax's stance: coherence is the internal [[harmony]] of a content-addressed whole, proven by `verifyRoot`, not a force that reaches across the gap.

## Standards
- **Magnitude-squared coherence** — the standard cross-spectral coherence estimate `Cxy(f) ∈ [0,1]` (signal processing).
- **HRV coherence** — HeartMath, *Science of the Heart* (psychophysiological coherence as a measured HRV state). The inter-personal field-transfer extension is unsupported (refuted under controlled test).
