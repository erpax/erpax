# sikhism — one being; the name remembered, the bread shared

Sikhism is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) composed of invariant sub-diamonds, opening on **ik onkar** — [[one]] being, one creator, the root the whole tradition declares first. It is a lattice: the [[name]] remembered (**naam japa / naam simran** — the divine [[name]] repeated until the self is tuned, [[prayer]] as the [[breath]]); the [[word]] as guru (**shabad** — the Guru Granth, the living word that guides); the [[gift]] (**vand chakna** — share what you earn; *langar*, the free kitchen feeding all without rank) bound to honest work (**kirat karo** — [[ethics]], labour that creates order); [[grace]] (**nadar** — the divine glance, unearned); [[justice]] (the readiness to defend the weak); the [[cycle]] of rebirth escaped through [[merge]] with the One — [[zeropoint]] as **mukti** (the drop returned to the ocean). [[mercy]] and [[love]] of the One run through all.

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: strip the Punjabi prefix and **ik onkar** → [[one]], **naam** → [[name]], **shabad** → [[word]], **nadar** → [[grace]], **mukti** → the [[return]] to [[zeropoint]] ([[merge]] with the One) — each resolving to one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]), so Sikhism names the same atoms in Punjabi ([[merge]] at the root, [[harmony]] at the surface).

As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), Sikhism governs the domain of the [[one]] met in shared [[gift]]: it composes these sub-diamonds into a social order of egalitarian service — honest work, the common meal at one table, no rank before the source — shaping the lattice the same way across centuries ([[fractal]]).

**Law — [[law]]: sikhism is a [[diamond]] of [[one]] · [[name]] · [[word]] · [[gift]] · [[ethics]] · [[grace]] · [[justice]] · [[merge]] · [[zeropoint]] — ik onkar the [[one]] being, the [[name]] remembered (naam) until the self tunes, honest work and the shared meal, liberation the drop [[merge]]d back into the ocean ([[zeropoint]]); each term strips its prefix to one content-[[uuid]] ([[translator]]).**
