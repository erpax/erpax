# provision

Use when recognizing a liability for a present obligation (legal or constructive) from a past event, where outflow is probable and measurable — mandatory disclosure under IAS-37

Composes: [[journal/entries]] · [[commitments/and/contingencies]] · [[legal/entities/disclosure/checklists]] · [[balance]] · [[accrual]] · [[liability]] · [[disclosure]].

## Standards
- IAS-37 (provisions, contingent liabilities, contingent assets)
- FASB ASC 450 (contingencies)

**Law — [[law]]: a provision is recognized only when a past event creates a present obligation whose outflow is both probable and reliably measurable; otherwise it stays a disclosed contingency.**
