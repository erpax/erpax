# jurisdiction

Use when a contract or matter specifies the governing law and venue — choice of law (which country/state), exclusive vs. non-exclusive forum, dispute resolution (courts, arbitration, mediation).

Composes: [[Contracts]] · [[matter]] · [[standard]].

**Law — [[law]]: a jurisdiction binds a matter to exactly one governing law and one forum (exclusive or non-exclusive) plus a dispute-resolution mode; this choice, not the substance, decides which court or arbitral body hears it.**

## Standards
- eIDAS-Reg-910-2014
- NY-UCC
- English-Law
- Rome-I-Regulation
