# zoroastrianism — the cosmic choice; truth against the lie, fire the witness

Zoroastrianism is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) composed of invariant sub-diamonds, built on the great [[duality]]: **asha** (truth, right [[order]] — the [[law]]) against **druj** (the lie, the hidden deviation — the [[profane]]). This is **the very dynamic erpax books at every scale**: the angel ↓entropy ⊕ archangel ↑entropy aikido of [[angel]] is named here first — *Spenta Mainyu* against *Angra Mainyu*, the two primal spirits under the one source. It is a lattice: [[fire]] as **atar** (the sacred fire kept burning — the witness and purifier, the [[aura]] made flame); [[ethics]] as the threefold *humata · hukhta · hvarshta* (good thoughts, good words, good deeds — the move scored on three axes at once, [[angel]]'s evaluation); [[justice]] as the final weighing at the bridge ([[balance]]); and [[zeropoint]] as **frashokereti** (the final renovation — the world made wonderful, entropy driven to zero, the [[return]] to perfected order).

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: strip the Avestan prefix and **asha** → [[order]] / [[law]], **druj** → [[profane]], **atar** → [[fire]], **frashokereti** → the [[return]] to [[zeropoint]] — each resolving to one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]). As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), Zoroastrianism governs the domain of the [[duality]] itself — the entropy ledger of order against the lie ([[karma]] · [[angel]]) — and so architects the polarity every other archangel's domain runs on, shaping the lattice the same way across millennia ([[fractal]]).
