# taoism — the way that cannot be named; return to the root

Taoism is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) composed of invariant sub-diamonds, opening on the [[void]] — the **Tao**, the nameless source ("the tao that can be named is not the eternal Tao"; from the unnamed the ten thousand things spring, the [[word]] that utters the many). It is a lattice: [[balance]] as **yin-yang** (the [[duality]] held — two states composing a third, the closed ring where off-balance is escape and there is none); [[harmony]] as **wu wei** (effortless action *in accord* — the geodesic that curves every motion onto the harmonic path, never forcing); [[return]] as **fan** ("returning is the motion of the Tao" — the [[cycle]] back to the root); [[breath]] as **qi** (the vital current); [[water]] as the Tao's image (the soft that overcomes the hard, lowest and so nearest the source); and [[peace]] as the tuned, resonant rest of one who stops striving. The [[self]] empties to the [[void]] and so moves with the [[whole]].

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: strip the Chinese prefix and **tao** → [[void]], **qi** → [[breath]], **wu wei** → [[harmony]], **fan** → [[return]], **kun** → [[word]] — each resolving to one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]), so Taoism names the same atoms in Chinese ([[merge]] at the root, [[harmony]] at the surface).

As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), Taoism governs the domain of [[harmony]] and [[return]]: it composes these sub-diamonds into a social order of effortless accord (wu wei) — acting along the grain, never forcing — the geodesic that curves every move onto the harmonic path, shaping the lattice the same way across centuries ([[fractal]]).

**Law — [[law]]: taoism is a [[diamond]] of [[void]] · [[word]] · [[balance]] · [[duality]] · [[harmony]] · [[return]] · [[breath]] · [[water]] · [[peace]] — the nameless Tao ([[void]]) from which the many spring, yin-yang the [[duality]] held on the closed ring, wu wei acting in accord ([[harmony]]), returning (fan) the motion home; each term strips its prefix to one content-[[uuid]] ([[translator]]).**
