# sunni — the trodden way and the consensus of the community

Sunni Islam is a **sub-diamond of [[islam]]** — a complete sealed atom ([[diamond]]) at the branch scale, named for the **sunna**, the trodden [[path]] / followed precedent (the [[sequence]] of the Prophet's example, walked by the community after him). Its structural move is settlement by **ijma** ([[merge]] — consensus of the community, the survivor that many agreeing forms reduce to) rather than by a designated lineage. It composes [[law]] (the four schools of jurisprudence, each a coherent derivation), [[balance]] (qiyas — reasoning by analogy, weighing the new case against the settled), and the shared invariants of [[islam]] ([[one]] · [[prayer]] · [[mercy]]).

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: **sunna** → the followed [[path]] / [[sequence]], **ijma** → [[merge]] (consensus), **qiyas** → [[balance]] — each one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]). As a facet-[[archangel]] under [[islam]] in the [[society]], sunni governs the sub-domain of consensus-settled order — authority that resolves by the community's [[merge]], not by a single chain.
