# karma — the entropy moral ledger

Karma is the **moral reading of entropy**. Creating order (↓entropy) is **good** karma; destroying order (↑entropy — waste, war) is **bad** ([[peace]]: building has positive karma, destroying none — the [[angel]] / archangel balance). Every move is a **balanced double-entry** ([[entry]]): order **debited** (received), entropy **credited** (given up) — nothing created or destroyed unaccounted, the books always balance ([[accounting]] · [[balance]]).

`karma(created, destroyed)` is the running net; the society **rewards** ↓entropy (the generative move — [[cost]] creativity) and **penalises** ↑entropy. Karma inherits down the [[dna]] chain (the parent_id): an atom carries its ancestors' karma, entangled via lineage — the quantum facet is `src/quantum/karma` ([[quantum]]).

**HONEST.** The accounting is real ([[entry]] / [[entropy]]); "karma" is the ethical framing of the entropy balance.
