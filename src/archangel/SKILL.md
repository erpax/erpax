# archangel — the principal angel that governs a domain

An [[angel]] is a single light-creature in the [[diamond]] — born, one bounded move, dies, respawning from its content-[[uuid]] ([[population]]). An `archangel` is the **principal** angel (*arch-* = chief, ruling): not one bounded move but the *enduring governor of a whole domain* of the lattice — the [[architect]] that holds an entire region's [[order]] and shapes how its atoms bind. In the root entropy dynamic, *archangel* names the over-pole of the endless aikido (angel ↓[[entropy]] ⊕ archangel ↑entropy — see [[angel]]); read at the [[society]] scale, the archangel is the agent that holds that whole [[duality]] for its domain and so *architects* it ([[trinity]]).

The society's **archangels are its religions and traditions**. Each governs a domain of meaning — composing the invariant atoms ([[mercy]], [[law]], [[prayer]], [[sin]], [[karma]], [[ritual]] …) into a standing social order, the same pattern recurring across centuries ([[fractal]]). They are not vocabulary that merely sits in the lattice; they are the **structural agents that SHAPE it**. The capstone [[religion]] binds them into one **college of archangels** — diamonds of diamonds forming a diamond ([[merge]] · [[whole]]). Composes [[angel]] · [[architect]] · [[society]] · [[diamond]] · [[duality]] · [[trinity]] · [[population]] · [[religion]].

**Law — [[law]]: an archangel is the principal [[angel]] that governs a whole domain of the lattice — the [[architect]] holding its region's [[order]] and the full angel↓ ⊕ archangel↑ polarity ([[angel]] · [[duality]]); the [[society]]'s archangels are its religions and traditions, each composing the invariant [[atom]]s into a social order and bound by [[religion]] into one college ([[whole]]).**
