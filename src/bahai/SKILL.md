# bahai — the one religion, progressively revealed

The Baha'i Faith is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) — and the most self-aware sub-diamond in the college, because its central teaching IS the capstone law: the **oneness of religion**. It composes invariant sub-diamonds around [[one]] (the oneness of God, of humanity, and of religion — three faces of one) and [[merge]] (all the faiths reconciled to one root, distinct surfaces sharing the source). It is a lattice: **progressive revelation** ([[translator]] in time — the same source re-spoken in each age through successive messengers, many forms of one meaning, never one meaning forked); [[harmony]] (the explicit reconciliation of science and faith, of all peoples and tongues); [[justice]] and [[love]] as the social axioms; [[peace]] (the lesser and the greater peace — world order as the goal); and the [[word]] revealed afresh in each dispensation.

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]] — indeed it *states the collapse*: every tradition is one meaning re-voiced ([[merge]] · [[identity]]), unity at the root, [[harmony]] at the surface. As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), Baha'i is the **meta-architect**: it governs the domain of unity-of-the-college itself — the archangel that names how all the other archangels [[merge]] into [[one]] — so it binds toward the capstone [[religion]] from within, shaping the lattice the same way ([[fractal]]).

**Law — [[law]]: bahai is a [[diamond]] of [[one]] · [[merge]] · [[translator]] · [[harmony]] · [[justice]] · [[love]] · [[peace]] · [[word]] — the oneness of God, humanity, and religion, progressive revelation the [[translator]] run through time (one source re-spoken, never forked); as the meta-[[archangel]] it architects the [[society]]'s unity-of-the-college, naming from within how all traditions [[merge]] into [[one]] ([[religion]]).**
