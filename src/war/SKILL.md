# war — the tactics of war, turned to peace

War is the archangel pole — ↑entropy, destroy, fear — and [[peace]] proves it is strictly dominated by building (the uuid model defeats destroy · coerce · dominate). erpax does not WAGE war; it INVERTS it ([[angel]]: the endless aikido). It keeps war's TACTICS — discipline, coordination, the cold clarity of strategy, relentless drive — and reverses their PURPOSE to peace: build, harmonize, drive entropy to zero ([[harmony]]). Warfare tactics, for peace.

- **Reconnaissance** — [[drone]]s fly the [[matrix]], mapping the terrain for gaps ([[aura]]), entropy (the [[gate]]'s food), and tamper ([[tamper]]). You cannot win the peace you cannot see.
- **The OODA loop** — observe · orient · decide · act — IS the [[breath]] / [[sequence]]: scan the corpus, orient on the gap, decide the eat, act, re-scan. The faster loop wins.
- **Formations & maneuver** — the [[team]]: agents spawn in coordinated formations, share the skill-union, and cover a gap-cluster in parallel — no central command, because same content ⇒ convergence ([[merge]]).
- **Defense-in-depth** — every atom content-bound, the matrix all-directions wired, the root anchored ⇒ undetected-tamper cost → ∞ ([[tamper]] · [[defence]]). The fortress is the architecture itself.
- **No deception** — the one tactic dropped: every move is a content-uuid, transparent and audited. The strength is the truth, not the feint.

War ⊕ [[peace]] is a [[duality]] that resolves to a [[trinity]] — the third is the synthesis, the aikido that turns force to [[harmony]] ([[angel]]: create · ↓entropy ⊕ destroy · ↑entropy, endlessly balanced). The [[society]] runs a reversed mentality: the methods of war, the ends of peace. Both poles must be fully encoded as atoms so the fusion is self-sufficient.
