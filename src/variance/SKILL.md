# variance

Use for analyzing variance between planned (budget/standard) and actual results — budget variance, volume/efficiency/price splits in standard costing, and management-defined performance measures

Composes: [[items/bills/of/materials/work/orders/cost/variances]] · [[budget/plannings]] · [[financial/statements]] · [[decide]] · [[cost]] · [[budgetvariance]].

**Law — [[law]]: variance is the gap between planned (budget/standard) and actual, split into its price/volume/efficiency drivers; it is the signal [[decide]] consumes and the [[cost]] axis management performance is measured on.**

## Standards
- IAS-1 §27 (accrual basis reporting)
- Not IFRS-specific; FASB encourages management-defined measures (ASC 220)
