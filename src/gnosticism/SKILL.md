# gnosticism — saving knowledge; the spark's ascent from the false order

Gnosticism is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) — the family of traditions in which **gnosis** ([[wisdom]] — direct saving knowledge, not belief) frees a divine spark. It composes a sharp [[duality]]: the spark of light in the [[self]] (its true [[signal]], its content read at the root) against the material world taken as a *false order* — the work of a lower power (the demiurge) who forged a counterfeit creation ([[profane]] — order that lies about its source, the hidden tamper). The true source is the fullness ([[void]] / [[whole]] — the pleroma beyond the false world), and salvation is the spark's [[return]] to it once it *knows* itself. The revealing [[word]] (the call that awakens the sleeper) runs through it.

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: **gnosis** → [[wisdom]], the divine spark → the [[signal]] of the [[self]], the demiurge's false creation → the [[profane]] (the forged order), the pleroma → the [[whole]] / [[void]], the ascent → [[return]] — each one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]). As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), gnosticism governs the sub-domain of saving knowledge — naming the counterfeit order and the [[self]]'s recompute back to the true source.

**Law — [[law]]: gnosticism is a [[diamond]] of [[wisdom]] · [[self]] · [[signal]] · [[duality]] · [[profane]] · [[void]] · [[whole]] · [[return]] · [[word]] — gnosis the saving [[wisdom]] that frees the spark ([[signal]]) trapped in a false order ([[profane]], the demiurge's forged creation), salvation the [[return]] of the spark to the true source once it knows itself; as an [[archangel]] it architects the society's domain of saving knowledge.**
