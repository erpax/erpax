# sacred — the verified-true (audit before act)

`sacred` is what passes the audit: the set-apart, canonical, zero-entropy ([[zeropoint]]) form whose content-[[uuid]] recomputes ([[proof]] — *verification*: it is what it claims to be), whose conservation holds ([[balance]], the [[accounting]] equation — *validation*: it is the right, conserved thing), and whose lineage traces to a real source ([[standard]] · [[akashic]] · [[history]]). The dual is [[profane]] — the lie, the manipulated datum, the forged ([[tamper/cost]]) record, the broken [[balance]], the dead link (an [[aura]] gap).

The pair plus the discerning act between them is a [[trinity]] — **sacred · profane · the audit** — and that trinity is run **at every scale, before acting**: a field, a posting, a period [[close]], an agent's [[contribution]], a federated [[merge]]. *Audit-before-act* is the same verification/validation the strict docs gate enforces (a dead link is profane — an unanswered question), the tsc/test gate enforces (red is profane), and the conservation laws enforce (an unbalanced ledger is profane). To find a lie is to **name** it: each form of manipulation mints its own detector under [[profane]] — verifications and validations without end ([[generate]] · [[aura]]: infinite skills and services).

## Traditions (prefix removed)
Every tradition divides **sacred ↔ profane** and *commands the discernment*: *qodesh* — "distinguish between the holy and the common, between the unclean and the clean" (Leviticus 10:10); "test the spirits, whether they are of God" (1 John 4:1); "test everything; hold fast what is good" (1 Thessalonians 5:21); "by their fruits you shall know them" (Matthew 7:16); the weighing of the heart against Ma'at's feather (Egyptian *Book of the Dead*); the scales, *al-mizan* — "no soul will be wronged… the weight of a mustard seed, We will bring it" (Quran 21:47). Eliade: the sacred is the *hierophany*, of a wholly different order than the profane; Otto: the *numinous*, the *mysterium tremendum et fascinans*, the Wholly Other. Composes [[proof]] · [[standard]] · [[balance]] · [[accounting]] · [[trinity]] · [[profane]] · [[identity]] · [[akashic]].
