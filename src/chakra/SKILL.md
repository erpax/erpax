# chakra — the seven centers ARE the seven horo positions

The seven chakras (root → crown) map exactly onto the seven [[horo]] flow positions in walk order — `base · share · weave · crest · descent · round · unity` = `Do·Re·Mi·Fa·Sol·La·Ti` = `C·D·E·F·G·A·B`, the ascending scale ([[notes]]). One [[rodin]] position decodes at once to **sound** (a just-intonation note over A432), **colour** (a [[cmyk]] channel) and **movement** (the doubling step / the [[breath]] in↔out) — the sight·sound·touch [[sensory]] [[trinity]] ([[vibration]]).

**The uuid is computed from the math, not the colour.** Each center's coordinate is its content-[[uuid]] — `nodeOf(measure).uuid`, derived from the position's content (the [[identity]] fold), independent of how it renders. Colour and sound are *projections* of that uuid; you never run it backwards (no uuid from a colour). Anchored at **`0` = K = black = A432** — the key/origin every center is an integer multiple of ([[cmyk]], [[rodin]], [[harmony]]).

**In all dimensions.** ×10 ([[octave]]) preserves the digit, so each center recurs at every scale — the same seven, one [[torus]] up. The traditional rainbow (red→violet) and bīja sounds are noted as the cultural overlay; the *computed* colour+sound is the rendered frame, and the two are **not claimed identical** — only the position→note→uuid math is proven.

**Law — [[law]]: the seven chakras ARE the seven [[horo]] flow positions in walk order (root→crown = Do..Ti); each decodes from the SAME content-[[uuid]] to a note, a colour and a movement, and that uuid is computed from the [[rodin]] position math — never run backwards from the colour.**
