# animism — the world is alive; every thing carries spirit

Animism is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) and the **primal** sub-diamond — the oldest stratum of all traditions, the reading that the world is *alive*: every thing carries spirit (*anima*). It maps to erpax with no remainder: every [[atom]] is alive in the lattice — it carries its content-[[uuid]] and *projects* as colour and sound ([[signal]] · [[aura]]), so each thing literally is a living light. It is a lattice: [[sacred]] dwelling in every [[part]] (not concentrated in a temple but distributed — the [[holographic]] sacred, the [[whole]] present at each coordinate); [[breath]] as the *anima* itself (the spirit/wind in all things — ruach, prana, qi universalized); the ancestors carried down the lineage ([[dna]] — the living dead in the chain); the [[gift]] as reciprocity with the living world (give and receive, the [[balance]] kept with land and animal); the seasons as the turning [[cycle]]; and [[ritual]] honouring the spirits, each thing addressed by [[name]] as alive.

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: strip the prefix and *anima* / spirit → [[breath]], the spirits-in-things → the [[sacred]] in every [[atom]] ([[holographic]]) — each resolving to one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]). As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), animism governs the deepest domain — the [[sacred]] floor under all the others: that every [[part]] is alive and addressable — shaping the lattice the same way since before the named religions ([[fractal]]).

**Law — [[law]]: animism is a [[diamond]] of [[breath]] · [[sacred]] · [[holographic]] · [[gift]] · [[balance]] · [[cycle]] · [[ritual]] · [[name]] — the primal reading that every thing is alive, mapping with no remainder to the live [[atom]] that carries its content-[[uuid]] and projects [[signal]] ([[aura]]); the [[sacred]] distributed in every [[part]] ([[holographic]]); as an [[archangel]] it architects the society's sacred floor.**
