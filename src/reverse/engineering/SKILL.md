# reverse/engineering — artifact back to the root

Forward engineering runs design → artifact ([[word]]s compressed into a binary, the [[gravity]] forward pull). **Reverse engineering runs the conjugate**: artifact → design, the binary expanded back into the word-chain at the root — the *reverse* pull [[gravity]] always carries, and the **inhale** of the [[breath]] (inhale a foreign system, exhale [[atom]]s).

erpax is *built* by reverse engineering. The corpus was [[migrate]]d by recovering two twenty-year Rails systems' DB logic into atoms (copy the data law verbatim, reimplement the scaffolding). The payment-rail map was reverse-engineered into the quantum frame. The [[security]] audit's red lens reverse-engineers a control to price the attack and [[harden]] it. One method, three ends.

## The method — the universal loop, run backward

1. **[[accept]] the artifact** — observe its behaviour, its structure, the schema it births. Do not assume; derive from what is really there.
2. **[[derive]] the words** — recover the generic concepts, one [[word]] each, stripped of the foreign framing and named by data-type.
3. **content-address + [[merge]]** — give each recovered concept its content-[[uuid]]; if it collides with an existing [[atom]], it *is* that atom (reuse, never re-mint). Recovery ends in the [[akashic]] record, not a fork.
4. **prove it in harmony** — the recovered design must regenerate the observed behaviour. That round-trip (artifact → words → artifact) is the [[proof]] the recovery is true — the same gate ([[confirm]]) every atom passes.

## The line — kept visible

Reverse engineering here serves **interoperability, migration, defensive [[audit]], and learning** — recovering structure to absorb, port, harden, or understand. It is not licence-bypass, piracy, or weaponisation; that is the [[fear]] direction, and the [[security]] twin prices it only to defend. Reverse to the root to *build*, never to forge.
