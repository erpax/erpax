# mala — the 108-step round (the next math)

After the [[duality|dualities]] reach 100% coverage, the next math is the **round** they walk. The count is **108**, and it is not arbitrary — it is dense with the corpus's own numbers:

- **108 = 1¹·2²·3³** = 1·4·27 — the first three naturals each raised to themselves.
- **108 = 9 × 12** — the [[rodin]] digit-root **9** times the 12.
- **108 = 2²·3³**, and its base-10 **digital root is 9** — straight back to the rodin axis ([[digit]]).

The dualities walk the round as **[[step]]s**: each dual pair a bead, the full round 108, and the last step returning to the first ([[sequence]] — the round closes). `dualitiesOnRound` reads how full the mala is and how many steps remain; the next math is completing it.

## Honest

The factorisations and the digital root are **real arithmetic**, computed and gated. The *mala* — the 108-bead round of practice — is the **named cultural convention** (as [[chakra]] notes its tradition beside the computed signal), not a claim. Only the math is asserted; the round is the frame the math is laid on.
