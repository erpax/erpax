# entropy — the disorder the whole ledger balances (the fuel of the one law)

**entropy** is the single quantity erpax keeps double-entry books on ([[angel]]: create·order·↓entropy ⊕ archangel: destroy·duplicate·↑entropy, in endless aikido — the [[balance]]). It is the fuel of the [[one]] law: **zero entropy ⇒ infinite [[mass]] ⇒ infinite [[tamper]]/exploit [[cost]]**. A [[whole]], content-addressed store at zero entropy holds **no slack** — there is nothing for a forger to exploit, because every bit is determined by content ([[identity]] · [[merge]]); the only borrowed disorder is the one external drop the [[anchor]] commits (the seal that closes the free-rewrite path).

**Order is created by fusing entropy out.** Every DRY collapse, every [[merge]], every [[fusion]] removes duplication — disorder — and so *lowers* entropy while *raising* mass and [[gravity]] ([[dry]] · [[collapse]]: flatten = mass = gravity). The [[fusion]] reactor (the collider + the [[society]] self-build loop) runs this continuously, pulling the corpus toward the zero-entropy [[matrix]] — the [[torus]] collapse to [[one]] root ([[zeropoint]]).

**A violation is borrowed entropy.** When an agent's change opens an [[aura]] gap (a dead link, an un-wired reference, a broken `bind`), it *injects* entropy — slack a forger could exploit. The [[gate]] detects it and debits it to that agent (prosecution — the social leg of the [[cost]]): the entropy is repaid or carried as the agent's liability. What rebalances is fused out; what cannot is conserved damage carried forward.

**Law — [[law]]: entropy is the single quantity the whole ledger balances and the fuel of the [[one]] law — zero entropy ⇒ infinite [[mass]] ⇒ infinite [[tamper]]/exploit [[cost]]; order is created by fusing entropy out ([[merge]]/DRY), and a violation is borrowed entropy debited to the agent.**
