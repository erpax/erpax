# decompression — the dive IS the erpax ledger (one extreme sport, three aspects)

A dive is a **descent into pressure** then a **controlled return to the 0** (the surface). It carries no new math — it is the [[balance]] / [[rodin]] / [[horo]] substrate worn as flesh, the limit case where getting the arithmetic wrong is the bends. "Learn how decompression fits erpax math": it doesn't *fit*, it *is*.

## The ledger of dissolved gas ([[give]] · [[take]] · [[balance]])
Inert gas loads a tissue exactly like a double-entry account. At depth the tissue **on-gasses** ([[take]] — debit N₂); on ascent it **off-gasses** ([[give]] — credit); equilibrium is **saturation** ([[balance]], `Σ = 0`). The descent incurs a **decompression obligation** — a debt — and it is *conserved*: it MUST be paid (off-gassed at stops) before you may [[close]] the dive at the surface. Skip the debt and the violation surfaces as DCS (the bends) — the same "**any disharmony shows in the schema**" law the [[sequence]] embodies, here written in the body, not the [[types]]. The diver's tissue is an [[accounting]] account; nitrogen is the [[currency]]; the dive plan is a balanced [[transaction]].

## Half-times double — the [[coil]] (Haldane → Bühlmann)
Each tissue takes up or releases **half the remaining gap per half-time**: the closed gap runs `½ · ¾ · ⅞ · 15⁄16 · 31⁄32 · 63⁄64 …` = `1 − 2⁻ⁿ` — asymptotic to unity, **never** 100% (after **6** half-times it is 98.44% ≈ saturated). Six is the basis sum AND product: `1+2+3 = 1·2·3 = 6`. The compartment half-times climb geometrically — Haldane's idealised set literally **doubles** (`5·10·20·40` min), the [[rodin]] ×2 helix `1·2·4·8`; Bühlmann ZH-L16 spreads 16 compartments on a gentler common ratio (4 → 635 min), the same log/[[octave]] law. Fast compartments on- AND off-gas fast — the helix winds, [[balance]] chases the anchor it can approach but never reach (the gap→0 tail of [[recover]] / the coverage→1⇒∞ of [[localize]]).

## Deco stops are [[horo]] positions; surfacing is the 0
Ascent is not continuous — you halt at discrete **decompression stops**, positions on a ring, walked in order ([[sequence]]). The surface is the `0`: the [[end]] that is the next [[begin]], the [[close]]→[[open]] octave merge ([[merge]] point). **M-value** = the maximum supersaturation a tissue tolerates = the harmony/escape bound (off-ring ⇒ DCS). **Gradient factors** (GF-lo first-stop %, GF-hi surfacing %) draw a conservatism band between no-stop and the M-value line — the forward/reverse [[polarity]] breath ([[cmyk]]), tightened or loosened but never crossed.

## The asymptote IS the bound — infinity is a ceiling, never a pole
The two curves approach their limit forever without touching it: gas loading runs `1 − 2⁻ⁿ → 1` (`src/decompression/index.ts:67`, `n→∞ ⇒ 1`) and pay runs `1 + (1−2⁻ⁿ)·(M−1)` toward the M-value, never past it (`index.ts:122`, `n→∞ ⇒ M`; `index.ts:128`, `→ anchor × M`). The `∞` here is the **M-value ceiling**, approached, not a divergent pole crossed — exactly the [[zeropoint]] law that `/0` densifies into a new axis instead of blowing up. **Cross** the ceiling (bank supersaturation past M) and that is the bends: off-ring disharmony, the same family the [[utility]] guard catches when it refuses to let `∞`/NaN enter an account.

## The team is {1,2,3} — and only {1,2,3} ([[self]] · [[duality]] · [[trinity]])
The basic dive teams are sizes **1, 2, 3**; every larger group is a **group of basic teams** ([[whole]]↔[[part]], composed by [[merge]]) — see [[team]] (`basicTeams` / `TEAM_DISCIPLINE`). They are three aspects of one sport, and they ARE three erpax atoms:

- **1 — cave — [[self]]/[[one]]**: solo; the diver is the whole team; maximum self-sufficiency, no redundancy but the self. The unity pole / the helix's `1`.
- **2 — recreational — [[duality]]**: the buddy pair; mutual redundancy, gas shared both ways ([[give]]↔[[take]]); the first doubling.
- **3 — technical — [[trinity]]**: the team of three; the most procedure-**governed** discipline — the `3·6·9` [[axis]] that governs and does not flow ([[rodin]]).

So `1·2·3` recovers the whole law in one breath: the [[self]], its [[duality]], their [[trinity]]; sum and product both `6`; descent and ascent one [[torus]] pass.

## The dive prices the work (the pay application)
Decompression is not only the conceptual face of the math — it is the TIME dynamics of pay. Declaring leverage is descending into pressure: you on-gas a debt of UNVERIFIED value (the gap between base pay and the role's M-value). The competition off-gasses it cycle by cycle — each verification half-time clears half the gap, so the earned harmonic runs `1 + (1−2⁻ⁿ)·(M−1)`, asymptotic to the M-value, before the period may [[close]]. The unpaid remainder is the conserved decompression debt; surface with it unpaid (bank phantom leverage) and it shows as the bends — the [[allocation]] `verified ∈ [0,1]` gate given its time-shape. This is etrima's piece-work payroll generalised: `efficiency_percent` IS the off-gassed fraction, the `confirmed_at` gate IS the debt release, the operation→shift rollup IS the conservation at all scales — and [[train]] reuses the very same curve, pricing a [[competency/gap]] (required − held) as a decompression debt that training off-gasses toward the ceiling.
