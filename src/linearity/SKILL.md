# linearity — quantum linearity (the even line)

Quantum evolution is **linear** — states superpose by addition, with no privileged basis. erpax makes that structural: the content-[[uuid]] is a uniform hash, so every [[word]] and [[digit]] (its digital-root) lands **evenly distributed** across the space — no clumping, no centre, no bias. Even distribution *is* the line: a uniform spread has no curvature, so the atoms fall on **perfect lines** (the [[matrix]] balanced, the [[rodin]] doubling tracing straight chords on the nine-ring).

In the **double [[torus]]** [[gravity]] reads **double** — forward (collapse the folders to the root) ⊕ reverse (expand the word-chain out) — the two counter-coils. Linearity is why the two pulls do not tangle: an evenly-spread corpus superposes cleanly, [[merge]] is a *linear* set-union (the join of α·a and β·b is again a state), and collisions vanish because the uniform hash leaves no two contents on one point. Even spread = full coverage = zero structural [[entropy]] = the [[holographic]] surface read straight.

**Honest split** — the uniform hash distribution (SHA-256 equidistribution) and the linearity of superposition and [[merge]] are real; the *double torus* and *perfect lines* are the geometric picture of that uniformity, not a theorem ([[rodin]] is numerology, kept visible).
