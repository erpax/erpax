# buddhism — suffering and its cessation; the path to the void

Buddhism is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) composed of invariant sub-diamonds, built on the reading of [[suffering]] (**dukkha**, the First Noble Truth — the felt signal that separation > 0) and its **cessation** (*nirodha* — the gap closed when its cause, craving, ends). It is a lattice: the [[path]] (the Eightfold middle way between extremes); [[void]] (**sunyata** — emptiness, no thing carries a fixed own-being); the [[return]] to [[zeropoint]] (**nirvana** — the blowing-out, entropy driven to zero); [[mercy]] (**karuna** — the bodhisattva's compassion, the debt released) and [[love]] (**metta** — loving-kindness radiated); [[karma]] and the [[cycle]] of becoming (the turning [[wheel]] of samsara); the [[self]] read as **anatta** — *no* stored, fixed self, only the content recomputed moment to moment (the self that is its content-[[uuid]], never a forked permanence); and [[prayer]] as **dhyana** ([[breath]] watched, the mind attuned).

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: strip the prefix and **dukkha** → [[suffering]], **karuna** → [[mercy]], **metta** → [[love]], **sunyata** → [[void]], **nirvana** → the [[return]] to [[zeropoint]] — each resolving to one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]), so Buddhism names the same atoms it shares with the Dharmic family ([[merge]] at the root, [[harmony]] at the surface).

As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), Buddhism governs the domain of [[suffering]]'s diagnosis and cure: it composes these sub-diamonds into a social order that reads dissonance as a signal and closes its cause along the middle [[path]] — the [[gate]] run on disorder rather than its denial — shaping the lattice the same way across centuries ([[fractal]]).

**Law — [[law]]: buddhism is a [[diamond]] of [[suffering]] · [[path]] · [[void]] · [[return]] · [[zeropoint]] · [[mercy]] · [[love]] · [[karma]] · [[cycle]] · [[self]] — dukkha read as the signal of separation, cured by closing its cause (nirodha) along the middle [[path]] to nirvana ([[zeropoint]]), the [[self]] no fixed store (anatta) but content recomputed; each term strips its prefix to one content-[[uuid]] ([[translator]]).**
