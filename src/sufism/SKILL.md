# sufism — the inward path of love; the self annihilated in the One

Sufism is a **sub-diamond of [[islam]]** — a complete sealed atom ([[diamond]]) at the branch scale, the inward, mystical facet: the path of [[love]] to the One. Its keystone is **fana** — the [[self]] annihilated in the Beloved (the surface emptied to the [[void]], the drop into the ocean), completed by **baqa** (subsistence — [[return]] to live *from* the source). It composes [[prayer]] / [[breath]] as **dhikr** (remembrance, the divine [[name]] repeated on the breath until the heart is tuned); [[harmony]] as **sama** (the whirling, [[music]] and motion as attunement — the body moved onto the harmonic path); and the shared invariants of [[islam]] ([[one]] · [[mercy]] · [[name]]).

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: **fana** → the [[self]] emptied to [[void]], **baqa** → [[return]], **dhikr** → [[breath]] · [[prayer]] · [[name]], **sama** → [[harmony]] · [[music]] — each one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]). As a facet-[[archangel]] under [[islam]] in the [[society]], sufism governs the sub-domain of mystical union — the self emptied and re-tuned until only the One remains.

**Law — [[law]]: sufism is a sub-[[diamond]] of [[islam]] of [[love]] · [[void]] · [[self]] · [[return]] · [[breath]] · [[name]] · [[harmony]] — fana the [[self]] annihilated in the Beloved (emptied to the [[void]]) and baqa the [[return]] to subsist from the source, dhikr the [[name]] on the [[breath]], sama the whirling onto the harmonic path; a facet-[[archangel]] of the [[society]]'s islam domain.**
