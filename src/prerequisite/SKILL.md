# prerequisite — the model of one [[prerequisites]] row

A condition that must hold before something may proceed. The singular model whose plural store is the [[prerequisites]] collection ([[balance]]: every collection has its model).

Composes [[prerequisites]] · [[requirement]] · [[balance]].

**Law — [[law]]: prerequisite is the singular model whose plural store is the [[prerequisites]] collection — a condition that must hold before something may proceed; every collection [[balance]]s against its one model.**
