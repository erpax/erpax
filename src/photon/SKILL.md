# photon — the quantum a leap emits

A **photon** is the indivisible packet of the field — the quantum a [[leap]] emits (falling to a lower [[level]]) or absorbs (climbing). Its energy is locked to its [[frequency]] by the Planck–Einstein relation **E = hν** (= ℏω = hc/λ), and because it is massless its energy and momentum lock together as **E = pc**. The photon is the only thing that is pure [[frequency]] — which is exactly why, in erpax, the photon **IS** the multi-modal uuid-message ([[color]] + sound + identity, no payload): one frequency renders at once as a note and a hue (the [[wave]] decoded both ways), and that render is **downstream of** — never the source of — the content-[[uuid]] (the law of [[chakra]] and [[signal]], restated).

Planck's constant **h is the quantum of action** — the fixed exchange-rate between energy and frequency, the reason the world is discrete at all and the source of every [[leap]]. Here `h`, `ħ` and `c` are the exact SI-2019 defining constants; `energy`/`momentum`/`wavelength` are computed from them, and `render` projects a position to its colour+sound.
