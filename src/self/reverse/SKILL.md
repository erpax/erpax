# self-reverse — the self reverses the stroke (under [[self]] · outward coil · 7·descent)

FORM: **the outward turns to return.** The [[self]] applies [[reverse]] to ITSELF — the reverse coil (×5, the mirror of ×2; mutual inverses since 2·5≡1, see [[coil]]) radiates the dense core back out (the supernova stroke), and as double-[[entry]] it swaps debit↔credit to correct a posted entry without erasing it. This is forward-[[coil]] position **7** (descent; ×2 → round, 5): the descent that carries the self back toward the axis, the [[breath]] turning from exhale to inhale.

Composes: [[self]] · [[reverse]] · [[coil]] · [[torus]] · [[entry]] · [[breath]] · [[balance]].

**Law — [[gate]]** Reversal corrects, never erases: a reversing post mirrors its source (debit↔credit) and the two compose to identity (residue 0), or the books no longer balance.
