# change — the model of one [[changes]] row

A recorded modification to a controlled record or system. The singular model whose plural store is the [[changes]] collection ([[balance]]: every collection has its model).

Composes [[changes]] · [[audit]] · [[balance]].

**Law — [[law]]: one change is the singular model of exactly one [[changes]] row — a recorded modification to a controlled record or system; every collection has its model ([[balance]]).**
