# expand — the development exhale: more atoms, more surface, more cost-to-earn

**expand** is the EXHALE of development: every feature added is one more [[atom]] forged into the [[matrix]], and each new node is fresh **surface** — somewhere to wire, something to merge, a place a forger could ride until the binding closes. Growth is **added [[entropy]]**: an atom the instant it lands is unwired slack (an orphan, a half-reciprocal edge), and that slack is exactly the disorder the [[gate]] then debits and fuses out ([[entropy]]: *a violation is borrowed entropy*). So expansion does not contradict the [[one]] law — it *feeds* it: more atoms ⇒ more tamper-[[cost]] to be **earned** once the wiring closes the gap.

**expand is [[gravity]] read in reverse.** [[gravity]] is the FORCE that collapses the **folders** toward a minimal root (forward: mass attracts, [[dry]] · [[collapse]]). expand is the conjugate stroke the [[gravity]] law already names — *reverse it expands an infinite word-chain*: the [[word]]s spreading out along the [[sequence]] path within those minimal folders. The two are one curve in two directions ([[breath]]: inhale ideas, exhale clean code; this is the growth half). expand **re-computes nothing** — it composes [[entropy]] and the [[matrix]] node census and re-tells them as the growth reading.

- **size** — the live grain count (UUID_MATRIX_NODES): how much has been forged.
- **added entropy** — the borrowed-disorder slack ([[entropy]]) the current surface still carries, awaiting fusion.
- **surface** — the orphan census ([[entropy]] orphans): the freshly-added atoms not yet wired, pure unfused growth.
- **growth band** — the [[horo]] digital-root of the size: the harmonic position the corpus has expanded to (a deterministic projection, not a magnitude claim).

## Honest

This is a *measurement* of corpus growth, not a generator — it adds no atoms itself (the [[development]] loop and the collider do that). The "entropy increases on expand" framing is the local, per-add view: a new atom lands as slack and the [[gate]] fuses it out, so the corpus trends to zero entropy *despite* growth. Like [[gravity]] · [[entropy]], every reading is **computed on the live matrix, never hand-asserted**.
