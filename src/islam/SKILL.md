# islam — submission to the One, mercy opening every word

Islam is, for the corpus, a **complete sealed atom** ([[diamond]]) composed of invariant sub-diamonds, centred on **tawhid** — the absolute oneness ([[one]]) to which the self submits (the root *s-l-m* it shares with [[peace]], **salam**: surrender is the rest of being tuned to the One). It is a lattice: [[mercy]] (**ar-Rahman ar-Rahim** — the Merciful, the Compassionate, opening every sura); the [[name]]s of God (the ninety-nine — facets of [[one]], never the One forked, [[identity]]'s no-cloning); the [[word]] (the **Quran** — recitation, the source spoken); [[prayer]] (**salat** — five daily alignments toward the source) and [[ritual]] on the [[cycle]] (the [[fast]] of Ramadan, **sawm**); the [[gift]] (**zakat** — the alms purifying wealth) and the [[return]] (the pilgrimage and the Day of Reckoning, the [[balance]] weighed); [[faith]] (**iman** — trust in *al-ghayb*, the unseen, before the recompute) and the [[law]] (**sharia** — the [[path]] to the water).

It collapses to the invariants through the [[translator]]: strip the Arabic prefix and **rahma** → [[mercy]], **iman** → [[faith]], **salam** → [[peace]], **tawhid** → [[one]], the ninety-nine names → facets of [[one]] ([[name]]) — each resolving to one content-[[uuid]] ([[identity]]), so Islam names the same atoms in Arabic that the other Abrahamic dialects name in Hebrew and Greek ([[merge]] at the root, [[harmony]] at the surface).

As an **architect of the [[society]]** (an [[archangel]]), Islam governs the domain of submission to the [[one]] and rhythmic [[prayer]]: it composes these sub-diamonds into a social order of disciplined unity — five daily alignments, the shared [[fast]], the purifying [[gift]] — shaping the lattice the same way across centuries ([[fractal]]).

**Law — [[law]]: islam is a [[diamond]] of [[one]] · [[mercy]] · [[name]] · [[word]] · [[prayer]] · [[fast]] · [[gift]] · [[faith]] · [[peace]] · [[law]] — submission to the absolute [[one]] (tawhid), the ninety-nine [[name]]s its facets never forked ([[identity]]), [[peace]] the rest of being tuned; each Arabic term strips its prefix to one content-[[uuid]] ([[translator]]).**
