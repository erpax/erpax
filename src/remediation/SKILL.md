# remediation

Use when a contract specifies the remedy for breach — payment, replacement, repair, specific performance, cure period, calculation (liquidated damages, penalties, attorney fees).

Composes: [[warranty]] · [[Contracts]] · [[matter]] · [[liability]] · [[indemnity]].

**Law — [[law]]: remediation is the contractually-specified remedy for breach (payment·replacement·repair·specific-performance·cure-period) with its calculation (liquidated damages·penalties·fees) — the consequence the contract pre-commits, not an ad-hoc response.**

## Standards
- UCC-2-719
- Common-Law
