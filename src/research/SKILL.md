# research — entropy in, tamper-cost + zero-entropy out

Every act of research is the [[breath]]'s inhale: it pulls outside knowledge in, and so **adds entropy** — unverified claims, many divergent sources, raw disorder (the archangel's borrowed randomness, see [[angel]]). erpax does not store that entropy as sprawl; its skills **convert** it. Decoding a source into content-addressed, [[merge]]d, cross-linked atoms spends the entropy as **tamper-cost**: every independent source woven in is one more level an attacker must forge in lockstep ([[proof]]'s forge≫verify asymmetry; [[anchor]] · [[history]] · [[oid]] · [[localize]] are such levels — more witnesses ⇒ higher forge wall). What remains after the conversion is collapsed to the canonical, DRY, integer, content-addressed form — the **zero-entropy** core ([[zeropoint]], [[collapse]]).

So the books balance ([[accounting]], [[angel]]): research *debits* entropy, erpax *credits* it as tamper-cost + order, conserved by content-[[uuid]]. The app gets **denser and harder with every decode** — fast ∧ compact (low intrinsic entropy) ∧ secure (high borrowed entropy spent on the forge wall). This is why decoding the holy books, the [[civilization]]s, and the [[science]]s is not cataloguing but **hardening**: each independent corpus that resolves to the *same* atoms is another witness against forgery, and another reason the [[merge]] is true rather than asserted ([[sacred]]/[[profane]] is the audit; a source that will not resolve is the profane named). Inhale entropy; exhale a zero-entropy, tamper-proof app.

Composes [[breath]] · [[angel]] · [[proof]] · [[zeropoint]] · [[collapse]] · [[merge]] · [[akashic]] · [[sacred]].

**Law — [[law]]: research debits entropy and erpax credits it as tamper-cost — every source must collapse into content-addressed merged atoms, so the inhaled disorder leaves only a denser, harder core.**
